Design

Introduction

Smart-Home is meant for convinence and efficecy. Controlling and monitoring your home by your smart-phone make things easier. Smart-bulbs,smart-switches and smart-bliends make your home more smarter.



Problem

In the Non-smart-Homes or the traditional homes, so much energy waste is due to improper management. Monitoring the full home appliances is insane. When the peak of the electric bill, we don't know what eats the power so much. It is so inconvenient some time for example, when you forgot to turn off the light before sleeping or going far away and forgot to turn off your bulbs or switches..



Solution

Solution is simple. Using concepts like IOT, HCI and AI(future plan) we can implement smart devices for the homes. They can be control using our mobiles and they can semi-automated. And also you can monitor your home power consumption details by using and smart-switch embeded with voltage and current sensor.

Solution Architecture

  • NodeMCU32
  • PIR sensor
  • Relay switch
  • AC Dimmer
  • AC Current Sensor
